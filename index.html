A hometown band with a dream to connect people of all ages through the power of music.


What began as a hobby, designed to pass the time among friends quickly grew into a passion project striving to inspire joy in others through music.
Directed by their 19 year old lead vocalist, Charline's Angels focuses on appealing to all ages through music that spans various decades, mixing of genres, and the unique bond found between their young singer and the rest of the band.
Performing various Pop/Rock, Blues, and R&B tunes, this band is sure to get you up on your feet with songs you know too well.
