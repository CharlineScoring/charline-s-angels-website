Meet the Band


Charline

Band Leader/Lead Vocalist/Pianist

Charline is the lead singer and pianist in the band.
Her music journey started with a couple keys on the piano at the age of 5, and from there she fell in love with music. She is currently studying Screen Scoring, Game and Interactive Media Scoring, and Musical Theatre Writing at Berklee College of Music in Boston. However, any time she comes home, she delights in playing music for her community with her favorite band.
